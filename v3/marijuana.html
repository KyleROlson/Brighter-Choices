Marijuana

Effects

  • Euphoria
  • Relaxation
  • Drowsiness
  • Impairs Short Term Thinking
  • Impairs Complex Thinking

Be Careful!

  • Marijuana has a history of being laced with much more potent drugs.
  • Depending on the moment, the high from marijuana can lead to depression and confusion.
  • In higher doses, it can cause hallucinations or become anxious or paranoid.

Long Term Use

  • Smoking: COPD when your tissue lining gets damaged and depending on the situation can lead to sudden death.
  • Eating: Feeling long term stomach aches, nausea and vomiting
